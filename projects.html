Selected Projects

A snapshot of recent and representative work across speech, AI, and linguistics.

Speech Analytics
FAANG Speech Analysis Project

LexAI Consulting supported a Fortune 100 initiative to enhance managerial communication through advanced speech analytics. The work focused on advising how vocal features—such as pitch, intonation, pacing, and emotional tone—should be modeled to improve real-time feedback evaluation.

Multilingual Content
AI Start-Up AI Generated Multilingual Text Project

LexAI Consulting partnered with an emerging AI start-up to strengthen the naturalness and authenticity of multilingual, AI-generated text. The engagement involved recommending key linguistic variation parameters to help the model produce more human-like, contextually appropriate output.

Forensics
Entrepreneurial Forensic Linguistics Project

LexAI Consulting contributed linguistic expertise to the development of an early-stage forensic intelligence tool for government and investigative use. The work expanded the system’s analytical scope beyond surface-level demographic cues by incorporating deeper linguistic indicators for profiling purposes.

Speech & Communication
Innovative Public Speaking Feedback Project

LexAI Consulting supported the design of a speech-feedback platform for professionals preparing for high-stakes presentations. The contribution centered on defining vocal performance metrics—such as rhythm, pitch, and speed to enable clear, actionable communication insights.
