About LexAI Consulting

LexAI Consulting is a boutique linguistic consultancy led by Dr. Alexia M. Hernández. The practice focuses on AI applications where language and speech are central: voice interfaces, audio-based systems, and language technologies that need to feel natural, trustworthy, and human-centered.

Drawing on deep training in linguistics and speech, Alexia helps teams design linguistic-centered workflows. This includes everything from the structure of prompts and dialogue flows, to the subtleties of prosody, style, and register in voice and text.

Her work bridges research and practice. On one side, she engages with questions of how language, identity, and power show up in speech and AI systems. On the other, she translates those insights into concrete design and evaluation decisions for product, policy, and research teams.

LexAI Consulting partners with organizations building voice and language products, evaluating AI-generated content, or navigating complex linguistic questions in emerging technologies. The goal is simple: systems that understand people better, and communicate back with more clarity, nuance, and care, and reflect the diversity of human voices.
